Our Role

The Stabilizing Force in Mushroom Production

MEXIMCO transforms a fragile agricultural product into a traceable, scalable, globally-ready supply chain.

Farmers

We partner with trusted mushroom farmers across Bangladesh, providing guaranteed purchase agreements and technical support to ensure consistent, high-quality harvests at fair prices.

MEXIMCO Hub

As the central processor, we stabilize raw mushrooms into premium, shelf-ready products, eliminating spoilage and price volatility.

Researchers

We collaborate with leading mycology institutions to continuously advance cultivation techniques, improving yields and contamination prevention through science.

Logistics

Smart routing and real-time demand data allow us to efficiently coordinate transport and storage, ensuring zero bottlenecks from farm to final delivery.

Markets

We deliver premium, traceable mushroom products to retail, HoReCa, institutional buyers, and international export markets, backed by full safety documentation.

Specialization

We focus exclusively on gourmet and medicinal mushrooms — Shiitake, Lion's Mane, Reishi, and Wood Ear for culinary, medicinal, and cosmetic applications worldwide.
