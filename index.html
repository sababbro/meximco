Who We Are

Building a Strong Foundation for Bangladesh's Mushroom Industry

MEXIMCO addresses a critical need in Bangladesh's mushroom sector: centralized processing and rigorous quality control.

By acting as a stabilizing hub, we solve challenges like short shelf life, inconsistent quality, and price volatility. We process mushrooms from partner farms into market-ready forms distributed based on real-time demand.

This approach protects farmers, builds buyer confidence, and drives sustainable industry growth.

Our Role

The Stabilizing Force in Mushroom Production

MEXIMCO transforms a fragile agricultural product into a traceable, scalable, globally-ready supply chain.

Farmers

We partner with trusted mushroom farmers across Bangladesh, providing guaranteed purchase agreements and technical support to ensure consistent, high-quality harvests at fair prices.

MEXIMCO Hub

As the central processor, we stabilize raw mushrooms into premium, shelf-ready products, eliminating spoilage and price volatility.

Researchers

We collaborate with leading mycology institutions to continuously advance cultivation techniques, improving yields, post-harvest handling, and contamination prevention through science.

Logistics

Smart routing and real-time demand data allow us to efficiently coordinate transport and storage, ensuring zero bottlenecks from farm collection to final delivery.

Markets

We deliver premium, traceable mushroom products to retail, HoReCa, institutional buyers, and international export markets, backed by full safety documentation and consistent quality.

Specialization

We focus exclusively on gourmet and medicinal mushrooms Shiitake, Lion's Mane, Reishi, and Wood Ear for culinary, medicinal, and cosmetic applications worldwide.

What We Do

From Farm to Table: Our Step-by-Step Process

01

Collection & Intake

Farm source, harvest date, variety, and quantity logged at every step for full traceability.

02

Sorting & Grading

Three grades: Premium (retail), Standard (HoReCa), Processing (value-add) zero waste.

03

Quality Standardization

Defect inspection, moisture control, and clean-room protocols ensure every batch exceeds expectations.

04

Processing & Value Addition

Dried forms, fine powders, and supplements extending shelf life from days to 12+ months.

05

Packaging & Labeling

Food-safe packaging with batch number, processing date, and storage instructions on every unit.

06

Market Distribution

Real-time routing to retail, HoReCa, enterprise, and export markets across Europe & North America.
