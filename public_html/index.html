Who We Are

Building a Strong Foundation for Bangladesh's Mushroom Industry

MEXIMCO addresses a critical need in Bangladesh's mushroom sector: centralized processing and rigorous quality control.

By acting as a stabilizing hub, we solve challenges like short shelf life, inconsistent quality, and price volatility. We process mushrooms from partner farms into market-ready forms distributed based on real-time demand.

This approach protects farmers, builds buyer confidence, and drives sustainable industry growth.

State-of-the-Art Lab

Our prototype facility features cutting-edge laboratory equipment and climate-controlled fruiting chambers, producing premium shiitake, lion's mane, and reishi mushrooms to the highest standards.

Research Partnership

We partner with leading mycology experts for continuous research and innovation, and collaborate with Royal Bengal Shrooms Limited (RBSL) for strategic branding and expanded market reach.

Premium Rebranding

Together, we are rebranding mushrooms from a small-scale crop into a premium nutrition powerhouse accessible, reliable, and trusted by consumers everywhere.
